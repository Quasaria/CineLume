Privacy Policy

Updated __BUILD_DATE_HUMAN_FR__

One-sentence summary: CineLume has no server, collects no personal data, uses no tracking cookies, no telemetry, no third-party analytics. Everything you do stays on your device.

What data is collected?

None. CineLume has no server or database. Your favorites, watchlist, custom lists, preferences (theme, language, filters, notifications) and search history are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage).

What external requests does the app make?

  • api.themoviedb.org — to fetch movie data (title, synopsis, cast, posters, trailers). Governed by TMDB's privacy policy.
  • image.tmdb.org — TMDB CDN for posters and backdrops.
  • www.youtube.com — only when you click to watch a trailer. Governed by Google's privacy policy.

No other external requests. None to any CineLume server (there is none).

Cookies and tracking

  • No cookies are set by CineLume.
  • No advertising pixel (Meta, Google Ads, etc.).
  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Plausible, etc.).
  • No application telemetry.
  • No browser fingerprinting.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications in Settings, CineLume uses the browser's Notification API, locally. No third-party push service is used. Notifications are triggered by the app's own code when you re-open the application.

Local storage

The browser's local storage (localStorage) contains:

  • Your favorites and watchlist (titles, TMDB IDs, release dates)
  • Your custom lists with their films
  • Your preferences (theme, language, active filters, spoiler-free mode, notification quiet hours)
  • Recent searches (last 8)
  • A visit counter (anti-spam for the PWA install banner)
  • A local TMDB cache to avoid re-fetching the same films

You can clear all of this via Settings → "Clear cache" or by clearing the site's data in your browser.

Export and portability

You can export your watchlist in Letterboxd CSV format at any time via Settings → Letterboxd → Export. No proprietary lock-in.

Open source

The full source code is available on GitHub under MIT license. You can verify these claims yourself.

Changes

If this policy is modified, the "Updated" date at the top will change. Version history is visible in Git.
